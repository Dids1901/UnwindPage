Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Diogo Musso Coutinho, doing business as Unwind ("Unwind", "we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use the Unwind mobile application and related services. For privacy law purposes, we act as the controller for the personal information processed to provide Unwind, unless a specific context states otherwise.

Unwind is a social entertainment app where users can discover, organize, rate, and share their experiences with movies, TV shows, and games. The app includes profiles, ratings, media status tracking, posts, comments, chat, notifications, reports, blocking, moderation, and gamified engagement features.

1. Information We Collect

Account and authentication information

We collect information needed to create and manage your account, such as email address, username, display name, authentication provider identifiers, password credentials for email/password accounts, and account status information.

If you use Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple, we receive limited information from those providers, such as your email address, account identifier, and, where provided, name. Apple may provide name and email only on the first login.

Profile and onboarding information

We collect profile information you provide, including username, display name, biography, avatar or profile photo, and date of birth. Date of birth is used to verify that users meet Unwind's 18+ eligibility requirement.

Entertainment and social activity

We collect and store information generated when you use Unwind, including ratings, media statuses, watched or played history, desired or consuming lists, favorite images, hype values, start and completion dates, posts, post images, comments, likes, diamonds, points, rankings, badges, feed activity, profile activity, and other engagement signals.

Communications and safety information

We collect chat messages, conversation metadata, notifications, reports, report reasons, moderation snapshots, block and unblock actions, follow relationships, and related safety records needed to operate social, moderation, and abuse-prevention features.

Device, usage, diagnostics, and analytics

We may collect device identifiers, app identifiers, operating system, device model, language, region, IP address, app version, session activity, screen views, feature usage, performance data, crash reports, logs, and diagnostic information. This data is used for security, troubleshooting, analytics, performance monitoring, and app improvement.

Permissions and files

With your permission, Unwind may access device features such as photos, media library, storage, camera-related file access, and push notifications. You can manage these permissions through your device settings. You can also turn off push notifications in your device settings.

Information we do not intentionally collect

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, health information, biometric identifiers, precise geolocation, or payment card information. Please do not include sensitive information in your profile, posts, comments, chat messages, or reports.

2. Sources of Information

We collect information from:

  • you, when you create an account, complete onboarding, post, chat, rate, report, or contact us;
  • your device and app usage, through Firebase, analytics, crash reporting, logs, and security tools;
  • authentication providers, such as Google and Apple, when you choose social login;
  • other users, when they interact with you, report content, follow you, block you, message you, or engage with your content;
  • third-party media data providers, such as TMDB and IGDB, for movie, TV, and game metadata that is not personal information about you.

3. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

  • create, authenticate, maintain, and secure accounts;
  • verify eligibility and enforce the 18+ age requirement;
  • provide profiles, ratings, posts, comments, chat, notifications, lists, feeds, search, and media pages;
  • display your activity to other users where the app's social features make it visible;
  • operate follows, blocks, reports, moderation, account deletion, and safety features;
  • send transactional, account, security, social, and feature-related notifications;
  • analyze usage trends, diagnose problems, improve performance, and develop features;
  • prevent spam, abuse, fraud, unauthorized access, and policy violations;
  • comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

4. Public and Social Features

Unwind is a social app. Depending on the feature and your activity, other users may see your username, display name, avatar, bio, profile activity, ratings, media statuses, posts, comments, likes, diamonds, points, rankings, badges, follows, and other social or entertainment activity. We do not display your email address, password, full date of birth, authentication identifiers, or private diagnostic data to other users.

Chat messages are intended for conversation participants and operational systems needed to deliver, protect, and moderate the service. Reports and moderation records are used for safety and enforcement and are not public profile content.

5. How We Share Information

We may share information:

  • with other users through social features you use;
  • with service providers that host, process, secure, analyze, deliver, or troubleshoot the Service;
  • with authentication providers when needed to complete login or account actions;
  • with app stores, operating systems, and device platforms as needed for app distribution and functionality;
  • with legal, government, or safety authorities when required by law or necessary to protect rights, users, or the Service;
  • in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or transfer of the Service, subject to appropriate protections.

We do not sell personal information and we do not use personal information for third-party targeted advertising.

We require service providers that process personal information for us to use that information only for authorized purposes and to apply protections appropriate to the nature of the information and their role.

6. Service Providers

Unwind uses third-party providers and SDKs to operate the app. These may include:

  • Firebase Authentication for account login;
  • Firebase Realtime Database for app data, social data, ratings, posts, chat, and caches;
  • Firebase Storage for uploaded images;
  • Cloud Functions for backend APIs, feeds, media lookup, moderation, deletion, notifications, and maintenance jobs;
  • Firebase Analytics for usage analytics;
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash and error reporting;
  • Firebase App Check and related security systems for abuse prevention;
  • Expo services for notifications, updates, and mobile app functionality;
  • Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple for optional authentication;
  • TMDB and IGDB/Twitch for movie, TV, and game data.

Information received from Google APIs is handled in accordance with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements, where applicable.

7. Legal Bases

Where laws such as the LGPD, GDPR, or similar privacy laws require a legal basis, we process personal information based on one or more of the following: performance of a contract with you, your consent, compliance with legal obligations, legitimate interests in operating and protecting the Service, and protection of users or other individuals.

For Brazilian users, the LGPD may provide rights such as confirmation of processing, access, correction, anonymization, blocking or deletion where applicable, portability, information about sharing, review of certain automated decisions where applicable, and withdrawal of consent.

8. Retention

We keep personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide Unwind, maintain accounts, operate social features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce terms, prevent abuse, maintain security, and keep backups. Retention periods vary depending on the type of data and the reason we need it.

9. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion in the Unwind app through Settings and Account where available. The app may require confirmation, such as reauthentication with your current login method, provider confirmation, or signing in again.

When deletion is requested, Unwind removes or de-identifies active account data and related social traces according to its deletion process. This may include your profile, posts, comments, likes, diamonds, chat inbox references, notifications, follows, blocks, media library data, ratings, and related indexes. Some cleanup may continue in the background after account removal.

Some limited information may be retained where necessary for security, fraud and abuse prevention, legal compliance, dispute handling, moderation records, backups, logs, or to complete the deletion process.

10. Security

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information, including Firebase security rules, authentication, access controls, App Check, moderation systems, and operational monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Transfers

We and our service providers may process information in Brazil, the United States, and other countries where our providers operate. These countries may have privacy laws different from those in your jurisdiction. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards or legal mechanisms for international transfers.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, restrict, object to, or withdraw consent for certain processing of your personal information. You can update some profile information in the app. You can also contact us to make a privacy request.

Withdrawing consent does not affect processing that occurred before withdrawal, and some processing may continue where we rely on another valid legal basis, such as providing the Service, complying with law, security, or abuse prevention.

We may need to verify your identity before responding. Some requests may be limited by legal, security, safety, anti-fraud, backup, or technical requirements.

13. Minors

Unwind is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly allow persons under 18 to create accounts or use the Service. If we learn that a user under 18 has provided personal information, we may suspend the account and delete the information as appropriate.

14. Tracking and Ads

Unwind may use SDKs and similar technologies for analytics, crash reporting, diagnostics, security, notifications, and app functionality. We do not use these technologies to display third-party ads, conduct third-party remarketing, or sell personal information.

15. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be identified by the "Last updated" date. If changes are material, we will provide notice where required by law or where practical.

16. Contact

For questions or privacy requests, contact us at contato@unwind.com.br. This is also the contact channel for requests from data subjects and communications related to LGPD privacy rights.
